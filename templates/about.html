About Me

I am a computational designer at Nervous System, where we use algorithms inspired by natura phenomena to generatively design products using digital fabrication.

Before my current job, I was a student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (twice!). As an undergraduate I focused in Physics while researching quantum computations architectures; as a Masters student I studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science while researching remote control stimulation of neurons using radio waves.

My interests are multi-disciplinary and span a wide number of fields. Some of them include:

  • instrumentation
  • control systems
  • physical computing
  • generative processes
  • digital fabrication
  • geometry
  • autonomous agents
